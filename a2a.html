August2August

A2A or August 2 August is a collection of poetry, thoughts, essays, and general nonsense from August 2019 to August 2020. The themes include topics of loneliness, connection, lesbianism, and the epitome of the "Early 20's". It currently exists in the form of a crumpled notebook, a very messy Google doc, and a SoundCloud.
